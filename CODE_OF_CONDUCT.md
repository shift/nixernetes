# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Nixernetes is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all community members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone participating in the Nixernetes community, whether online or in person.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Being patient and understanding with those asking questions
- Helping newer members feel welcome

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Deliberately disruptive behavior
- Gatekeeping - preventing others from participating based on assumptions about experience level

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to:

- Remove, edit, or reject contributions (comments, commits, code, issues, discussions) that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful
- Explain the reasons for moderation decisions in a transparent manner when appropriate

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **On GitHub**: Use the Report button on comments/discussions, or mention `@admins` in an issue
2. **Via Email**: Contact conduct@anomalyco.com with:
   - Description of the violation
   - Date and time it occurred
   - Links to the relevant content (if applicable)
   - Your contact information
3. **In Person**: Speak with organizers if this is an in-person event

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality. We will not tolerate retaliation against anyone reporting a violation in good faith.

## Investigation Process

When we receive a report:

1. We acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. We investigate the allegation thoroughly
3. We may contact involved parties for more information
4. We determine appropriate action based on our findings
5. We communicate our decision to the reporter

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All Nixernetes GitHub repositories and discussions
- Official Nixernetes events (virtual or in-person)
- Official community chat channels
- Any official Nixernetes-branded spaces

This Code of Conduct also applies when an individual is representing the Nixernetes community in public spaces.
